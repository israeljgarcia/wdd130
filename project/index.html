Overview

Purpose

Dry Oar has been a dream of mine since I was a little boy. I have always loved
white water rafting and this company is my way of bringing joy to others who also
enjoy it. Whether it's you and your friends or you and your family, we have packages perfect for any occasion.

Audience

These activities are best enjoyed with a group of friends or with family
from ages 8 and up.

Typography

Heading Font: Source Code Pro

I want to choose this font for the heading because it looks more formal and professional,
but it still fits my theme.

Paragraph Font: Indie Flower

I chose this font because it is playful and it is attractive to read
unlike Times New Roman which makes me think of essays.
